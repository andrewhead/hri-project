Instructions

To find the target image, you must do the following. The algorithm will show you a row of images it has selected. You must drag the examples to order them from best to worst.

When you're happy with the ordering, click I Have Sorted the Images. The algorithm will fetch more examples for comparison.

The task will end when the algorithm has given you the target image, and you have selected it as the best one. In some cases, after many comparisons, the algorithm may not reach the expected goal. If the algorithm seems to "get stuck", click on the "Images Aren't Getting Any Better" button to end the task. This button appears after 10 comparisons to give the algorithm a chance to show enough examples. You will still get compensated for this HIT if you click this button.

All of these instructions will be available when you complete the task
